About me and the game.

Game is inspired by an old Persian card game called Hokm.
we have four types of cards,called suits in English.They are:

Diamonds ♦ represents wealth or material things.
Hearts ♥ usually represents love or emotion.
Spades ♠ represents wisdom or challenges.
Clubs ♣ represents growth or action.
